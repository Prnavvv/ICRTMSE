About SVKM

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM) is a Public Charitable Trust registered under the Society’s Registration Act and Bombay Public Trust Act. From its humble beginnings in 1934, when it took over the Rashtriya Shala, a school established in 1921 in the wake of the National Movement, the Mandal today has grown into a big educational complex imparting high-level education to students. For more information, click here.

About NMIMS

In 1981, Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM) established Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) to meet the growing demand for management education. In 2003, NMIMS was declared a deemed to be university under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. The Vision of the University is to be a globally admired University by 2030. The Mission of NMIMS is to emerge as a Centre of Excellence, best in class in India and Asia and yearning to be the best in the world by 2030. For more information, click here.

About STME

School of Technology Management and Engineering (STME), Indore is a constituent school of SVKM's NMIMS which is a Tier-1 University under Autonomous Universities category according to University Grants Permission (UGC), India. It was founded in the year 2017 on the principles of imparting quality education to students and grooming them to be practitioners of what they are taught. The evolution of the school as an institution of prominence in the country today primarily due to the strong industry participation in all strategic and decision making processes further strengthening the high academic standards that management of SVKM have set. The consistent academic quality, research focus, faculty from top national and global institutes and strong industry linkages at STME has placed it amongst the nation’s prime centers of educational excellence and research today. About ICRTMSE

The International Conference on Recent Trends in Mathematics, Statistics, and Engineering (ICRTMSE) is a conference that provides a forum for professionals, academics, and researchers from all over the world to exchange the most recent findings, innovations, and insights in the areas of mathematics, statistics, and engineering. The conference aims to promote interdisciplinary research, develop collaboration, and extend the limits of knowledge in these crucial areas. This conference is features a diverse range of sessions, including keynote addresses, plenary talks, and parallel technical sessions. Renowned experts and thought leaders in mathematics, statistics, and engineering are invited to deliver keynote speeches, providing attendees with valuable perspectives on emerging trends and challenges in these fields. These addresses often touch upon the intersection of these disciplines, highlighting the interconnectedness and the potential for cross-disciplinary collaborations.

Submission

Authors are encouraged to submit their research papers, case studies, and innovative ideas aligned with the conference theme and tracks initially in any standard format. Abstracts of length limited to 2500 characters submit through the online submission portal Microsoft CMT. The steps required to finish the submission procedure are as follows:

  • Step 1: Submit your abstract through the online submission portal Microsoft CMT. Here is a link to submission assistance. Microsoft CMT
  • The corresponding author of the accepted abstract will be notified through an email and will be asked to the register for the oral presentation and submit the full length paper for review purpose.
